Unit 2: Exploratory Data Analysis

I studied the foundational elements of exploratory data analysis (EDA) this week. I gained knowledge of the essential procedures for comprehending a dataset, such as:

  • Feature exploration: Learning more about the traits of specific features in the dataset.Finding odd or unexpected data points that could distort the study is known as anomaly detection.
  • Visual Analysis: Finding patterns, trends, and connections by using a variety of visualization tools.

I gained a better grasp of how to get a dataset ready for machine learning tasks as a result of this investigation. Effective data analysis enables me to make well-informed choices regarding feature engineering, data cleansing, and model selection.

All things considered, the skills I've learned this week have given me a strong basis for carrying out efficient EDA and making sure my machine learning models are based on accurate and pertinent data.
